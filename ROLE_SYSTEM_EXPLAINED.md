## 👥 User Roles

### 1. 👤 Customer (Default for Everyone)

**How to become:** Just sign up!

**What they can do:**
- Order rides immediately
- Order supermarket deliveries
- Track location in real-time
- Pay via multiple methods
- Rate and review riders
- View ride history

**Platform Access:** Full customer dashboard immediately after signup

---

### 2. 🏍️ Rider

**How to become:** 
1. ✅ Sign up first (becomes customer)
2. ⏳ Wait for Stage Chairperson or Developer to assign rider role
3. ✅ Once assigned, access rider platform

**What they can do:**
- Accept ride requests
- Complete deliveries
- Earn 70% of each fare
- Track earnings
- View commission breakdown
- Update availability

**Platform Access:** Only after being assigned by Stage Chairperson

---

### 3. 👔 Chairperson (5 Levels)

**How to become:**
1. ✅ Sign up first (becomes customer)
2. ⏳ Wait for higher-level Chairperson or Developer to assign
3. ✅ Once assigned, access chairperson platform

**Levels:**
- **District Chairperson** - Manages entire district
- **Division Chairperson** - Manages division
- **Subcounty Chairperson** - Manages subcounty
- **Parish Chairperson** - Manages parish
- **Stage Chairperson** - Manages stage (can assign riders)

**What they can do:**
- Assign lower-level chairpersons
- View region statistics
- Track commission earnings
- Approve riders (Stage Chairpersons)
- Withdraw commissions

**Platform Access:** Only after being assigned by authorized user

## 🎯 Key Rules

### ✅ DO's
- **Anyone** can sign up and order rides/deliveries immediately
- **Riders** must be assigned by Stage Chairperson or Developer
- **Chairpersons** must be assigned by higher-level or Developer
- **Users must sign up first** before being assigned any role

### ❌ DON'Ts
- Users **cannot** self-promote to Rider or Chairperson
- Stage Chairpersons **cannot** assign District Chairpersons
- Riders **cannot** assign other riders
- Customers **cannot** access rider/chairperson platforms

## 📞 Common Questions

**Q: Can I sign up and become a rider immediately?**
A: No. You sign up as customer (can order rides), then wait for assignment as rider.

**Q: Who can assign me as a rider?**
A: The Stage Chairperson of the stage you want to join, or the Developer.

**Q: Can I be both a customer and a rider?**
A: Your role changes. Once assigned as rider, you have rider platform access (but can still use customer features).

**Q: How do I assign riders as Stage Chairperson?**
A: In your chairperson dashboard, go to "Manage Riders" and promote registered customers.

**Q: Can customers order deliveries from supermarkets?**
A: Yes! Immediately after signup, customers can order rides and supermarket deliveries.
